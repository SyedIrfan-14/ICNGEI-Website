About ICNGEI 2025

The Conference on NextGen Electronics and Innovation (ICNGEI) covers emerging trends and advancements in electronics, including AI integration, IoT, nanoelectronics, and sustainable practices. It focuses on advanced circuit design, wireless communication, power electronics, and digital transformation, highlighting the role of electronics in automation and robotics. The conference provides a platform for experts to share research, insights, and future perspectives in these key areas.

SRM Institute of Science and Technology

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly SRM University) is a top-ranking university in India, with over 20,000 students and 1,500 faculty members. The institute offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across Engineering, Management, Medicine, Health Sciences, and Humanities.

Department of ECE

The ECE program at SRM is designed to establish a strong engineering foundation while offering electives that allow specialization in various technology areas. The department has MoUs and industrial partnerships with companies like Nokia, Flexol, CEERI, and FLDEC. It also collaborates with professional bodies such as IETE, IEI, IEEE, and IET.

The department runs clubs like ‘Electrum,’ the Robotics Club, and the start-up club ‘Aarambh.’ It also manages a wall magazine called ‘Adhyaay,’ which showcases student articles, poems, and artwork. Workshops on ML, AI, Embedded Systems, and National-Level events like ‘ABIGHYAN,’ ‘REACT,’ and ‘XION’ are regularly conducted.
